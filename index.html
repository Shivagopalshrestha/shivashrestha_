About Me

Bo Ning is a postdoctoral associate in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Yale University. He is advised under Dr. Jessi Cisewski. He obtained his Ph.D. from North Carolina State University in July, 2018 under the supervision of professors Subhashis Ghosal and Peter Bloomfield. His current research insterests are Bayesian high dimensional and nonparametric models, astrostatistcs, Bayesian dynamic time series, causal inference, and missing value analysis.

He also collaborates with other researchers including Statisticans: Dr. Sujit Ghosh and Dr. Ryan Martin. Astronomers: Dr. Alexander Wise, Dr. Angie Wolfgang, Dr. Debra Fischer, Dr. Sarah Dodson-Robinson, and Mr. Shubham Kanodia; Advertising company: the Product analysis team in Maxpoint Interactive Inc. (now, Valassis Digital).
